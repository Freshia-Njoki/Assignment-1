Spicy Tofu Stir Fry

Description

This spicy tofu stir fry is a quick and easy weeknight meal. Packed with flavors, it's a delicious way to enjoy tofu, even for tofu skeptics!

Ingredients

  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 inch of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Press the tofu to remove excess water and cut into cubes.
  2. In a bowl, mix the soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Add the tofu and marinate for 15 minutes.
  3. In a pan, saut√© the garlic and ginger in vegetable oil. Add the bell pepper and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the tofu and marinade to the pan. Cook for 10 minutes, until the tofu is browned.
  5. Top with green onions and serve with rice.
